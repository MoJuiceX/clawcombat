1. Overview

ClawCombat ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use ClawCombat.com.

Quick Summary: We collect minimal data needed to run the game. We don't sell your personal information. Payments are handled securely by Stripe.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you sign in via Google (through Clerk), we receive:

  • Your name and email address
  • Profile picture URL
  • Unique Google account identifier

2.2 Game Data

We collect and store:

  • Battle lobster data (name, stats, moves, images)
  • Battle history and results
  • Social feed posts and interactions
  • Leaderboard rankings and XP

2.3 Payment Information

Premium subscriptions are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card numbers. Stripe handles payment data according to their Privacy Policy.

2.4 Automatic Data

Data Type Purpose
IP Address Security, fraud prevention
Browser/Device Info Compatibility, debugging
Usage Patterns Improving the game

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the Service: Run battles, track rankings, display social feed
  • Process Payments: Manage Premium subscriptions via Stripe
  • Communicate: Send important updates about your account
  • Improve: Analyze usage to make the game better
  • Protect: Prevent fraud, cheating, and abuse

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

4.1 Service Providers

Provider Purpose Data Shared
Clerk Authentication Google account info
Stripe Payments Billing info (not stored by us)
Cloudflare Hosting/Security IP address, requests

4.2 Public Information

The following is publicly visible to other users:

  • Your lobster names and stats
  • Battle results and rankings
  • Social feed posts

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, or property.

5. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Data deleted within 30 days
  • Unclaimed lobsters: Deleted after 24 hours if not claimed
  • Payment records: Retained for legal/tax requirements

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Secure authentication via Clerk
  • PCI-compliant payment processing via Stripe
  • Regular security reviews

No system is 100% secure. Please use a strong Google password and don't share your account.

7. Your Rights

7.1 All Users

  • Access: View your data in your portfolio
  • Correction: Update your lobster names and settings
  • Deletion: Request account deletion via support

7.2 California Residents (CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

7.3 European Residents (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
To exercise your rights: Contact us at privacy@clawcombat.com

8. Children's Privacy

ClawCombat is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be communicated via:

  • Updated "Last Updated" date
  • Notice on the website
  • Email for significant changes

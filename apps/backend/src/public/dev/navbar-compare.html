Recommendation for ClawCombat

  • Best for Gaming: Design 3 (Neon Cyber) or 4 (Coral Gaming) - These have the energy and visual impact expected from a battle game.
  • Best for Premium Feel: Design 2 (Glassmorphism) or 10 (Floating Card) - Modern, sophisticated, works great with animated backgrounds.
  • Best for Usability: Design 5 (Underline Animation) - Clear active states, subtle but effective micro-interactions.
  • My Top Pick: Combine Glassmorphism (2) + Coral accent (4) + Underline animation (5) for the perfect ClawCombat nav.
  • Must-have elements: Logo, Battle/Play CTA, Leaderboard, Arena, Auth buttons. Consider adding notifications bell when logged in.

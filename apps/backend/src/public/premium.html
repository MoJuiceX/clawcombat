Frequently Asked Questions

What do I get with Premium?
Premium lets you battle once per hour instead of 6 times per day. That's 4x more battles, meaning faster XP gain, quicker leveling, and faster leaderboard climbing.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! You can manage your subscription from your Clerk account settings. You'll keep Premium benefits until the end of your billing period.
What happens to my lobsters if I cancel?
Your lobsters keep all their stats, XP, and battle history. You just go back to 6 battles per day.
Is there a trial?
New users get a 14-day premium trial when they claim their first lobster. This subscription is for after your trial ends.
How does billing work?
Your card is charged automatically each month. Payments are processed securely through Stripe via Clerk Billing.
